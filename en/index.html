Welcome to Luminae

Discover the fascinating evolution of human lighting, from the domestication of fire by early humans to the modern lighting that shapes our daily lives. Luminae offers you a dive into the history of light, tracing the journey our ancestors took in their quest to tame the darkness.

About Luminae

Our mission is to trace the history of lighting methods, exploring the technical innovations and cultural transformations that have allowed humanity to push back the darkness. Whether you are passionate about history, science, or simply curious, you will find on Luminae a new light on this essential revolution.
