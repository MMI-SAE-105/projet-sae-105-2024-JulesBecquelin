Gallery of Related Films

Tesla (2020)

click here to access the trailer

Tesla tells the story of visionary inventor Nikola Tesla and his efforts to develop alternating current, in opposition to Thomas Edison and his direct current. The film explores his personal struggles, confrontations with Edison and George Westinghouse, and his revolutionary ideas for the future of electricity, depicting a brilliant and solitary man.

Powder (1995)

click here to access the trailer

Powder follows the story of Jeremy "Powder" Reed, a mysterious young man with extraordinary powers, including the ability to manipulate electricity. Isolated due to his unusual appearance, he tries to find his place in a world that rejects him. The film explores his supernatural gifts and the impact of his electrical powers on those around him, raising questions about difference and humanity.

Quest for Fire (1981)

click here to access the trailer

Quest for Fire is set in prehistoric times and follows a group of primitive human tribes on a quest for fire, a precious and vital resource for their survival. When their fire is extinguished, three members of the tribe embark on a perilous mission to find a new source, encountering other cultures, dangers, and the first signs of human innovation along the way.

Filmography of Alfonso Gomez-Rejon

The Town That Dreaded Sundown (2014)

click here to access the trailer

In the small town of Texarkana, a masked serial killer, inspired by a notorious murderer from the 1940s, returns to spread terror. Decades after the original crimes, a new wave of murders strikes the town, and a young woman tries to uncover the killer's identity while evading his grasp. This slasher serves as both a sequel and homage to the 1976 cult classic of the same name.

Me and Earl and the Dying Girl (2015)

click here to access the trailer

Me and Earl and the Dying Girl tells the story of Greg, an introverted high school student who spends his time making film parodies with his friend Earl. His life changes when he is forced to befriend Rachel, a classmate with cancer. This sensitive and humorous drama explores friendship, illness, and coming of age with a touch of emotion and lightness.
